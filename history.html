History

Architectural culture

There are many hakka dwellings in Meizhou, such as the Wai Long House, the Horse house, the Five-Phoenix House, the Tuwai house, the four-turret, the multi-bar house, the Chinese and Western mixed arcade building, the turret, and the diaolou.

Overseas Chinese hometown culture

Meizhou is one of the most representative hakka settlements in the world. It is the last point of departure for hakka people to migrate south and the main starting point for them to spread across the world. Together with Southern Jiangxi and western Fujian, meizhou is known as the Hakka base in the Border region of Fujian, Guangdong and Jiangxi.

In 2013, China (Meizhou) Immigrant Memorial Square, the only project in China to commemorate immigrants established by UNESCO, was officially inaugurated in Songkou Town, Mei County.At the same time, the World Immigration Exhibition hall also opened to welcome visitors.
