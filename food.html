Specialty food

Hakka specialty

Meizhou is rich in special products, such as Meizhou golden pomelo, Pingyuan Navel orange, Hakka Niang wine, Changle shochu wine, Wuye God, Tai Po dried bean curd, Dundong tea, Xingning tea oil, Ba Xiang Mountain sweet potato and other famous specialties.

Hakka cuisine

Meizhou is a home to Hakka cuisine. There are many hakka delicacies in Meizhou pickled noodles, baked chicken with salt, fermented tofu, braised pork with plum dishes, beef balls from Hakka countries, green dumplings, yellow dumplings and sweet dumplings.
