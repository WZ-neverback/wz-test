Basic Information

Location

Meizhou, a prefectural city under the jurisdiction of Guangdong Province, is located in the northeast of Guangdong province, bordering fujian Longyan and Zhangzhou in the east, Chaozhou, Jieyang and Shanwei in the south, Heyuan in the west, and Ganzhou in Jiangxi province in the north.Jurisdiction meijiang district, Meixian district, Pingyuan County, Jiaoling County, Tai Po County, Fengshun County, Wuhua County, xingning city.

Community

Meizhou is not only the last place for hakka people to migrate to the south, but also the main starting point for hakka people to spread to the whole world since the Ming and Qing Dynasties. There are more than 7 million overseas Chinese and compatriots living in Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan. It is the key hometown of overseas Chinese in China and one of the important ancestral homes of Compatriots in Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan.

Reputation

Meizhou is a famous national historical and cultural city, an excellent tourist city in China, and the most representative hakka settlement in the world. It is known as the "world guest capital", the hometown of Marshal Ye Jianying, the famous old revolutionary base area, and the cross-Straits exchange base.It is the famous hometown of Chinese culture, overseas Chinese, football, mountain songs, golden pomelo, Hakka dishes, Single cong tea, camellia, Han Music, hot springs, longevity and Chinese poetry.

Mountains

Meizhou is located in the south of The Wuling Mountains. The terrain is high in the north and low in the south. The mountains are mainly composed of wuyi Mountains, Lotus Mountains and Phoenix Mountains.There are more than 140 peaks above 1000 meters above sea level, among which Tongguzhang in Fengshun County is 1560 meters above sea level, the first peak in Meizhou.
