Introduce

Meizhou is rated as "the most popular destination for motorists" and "the best leisure city" in Guangdong.In 2013, 18 scenic spots including Ye Shuai's hometown were selected as the "18 Scenes of Meizhou".In 2017, ye Shuai's former residence was selected as one of the "Top 10 Cultural Landmarks of Hakka".And evaluated "Gu Mei county eight scenes".There are many famous mountain temple, cultural relics, former residence of celebrities, hot spring waterfall, beautiful lake mountain.By the end of 2017, there are 128 major scenic spots in the city, among which 33 are rated aaa and 105 others have been opened or under construction.
